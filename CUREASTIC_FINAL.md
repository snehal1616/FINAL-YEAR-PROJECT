- **Background:** Cyan gradient (#06b6d4 → #0ea5e9)
- **Shadow:** Soft, elevated (`0 10px 30px rgba(6, 182, 212, 0.3)`)
- **Hover Effect:** Lifts up with enhanced shadow

#### Logo Text:
- **Content:** "Cu" (Cureastic abbreviated)
- **Font:** Inter (clean, modern)
- **Weight:** 700 (bold but not heavy)
- **Size:** 1.5rem
- **Color:** White
- **Medical Symbol:** Small "✚" in top-right (subtle, 30% opacity)

### Color Palette:
- **Primary Cyan:** #06b6d4 (fresh, medical, clean)
- **Secondary Cyan:** #0ea5e9 (vibrant, trustworthy)
- **White:** #ffffff (purity, clarity)
