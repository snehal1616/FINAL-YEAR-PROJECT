## 🌦️ Weather Intelligence Examples

### Hot Weather (>30°C)
```
BEFORE:
"Stay hydrated."

AFTER:
⚠️ Health Risk:
• High temperatures (35°C) can cause heat exhaustion and heatstroke
• Dehydration risk is elevated

💡 Advice:
• Drink 3-4 liters of water throughout the day
• Avoid outdoor activities between 11 AM - 3 PM
• Wear light-colored, loose-fitting clothes
• Watch for signs: dizziness, rapid heartbeat, confusion

🏥 When to See a Doctor:
• Body temperature above 104°F
• Confusion or altered mental state
• Rapid breathing or racing heart
```

### Rainy Weather
```
BEFORE:
"Be careful of infections."

AFTER:
⚠️ Health Risk:
• Rainy season increases risk of dengue and malaria
• Stagnant water breeds mosquitoes
• Waterborne diseases are common

💡 Advice:
• Use mosquito repellent and nets
• Eliminate standing water around your home
• Drink only boiled or purified water
• Wash hands before eating
• Cover food to prevent contamination

🦟 Dengue Warning Signs:
• High fever with severe headache
• Pain behind eyes
• Joint and muscle pain
• Rash appearing 2-5 days after fever
```

### Cold Weather (<18°C)
```
BEFORE:
"Keep warm."

AFTER:
⚠️ Health Risk:
• Cold weather (12°C) increases flu and respiratory infections
• Hypothermia risk if exposed for long periods
• Weakened immune system

💡 Advice:
• Dress in layers to maintain body heat
• Drink warm fluids like soup and herbal tea
• Wash hands frequently (20 seconds with soap)
• Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours) to boost immunity
• Exercise indoors to stay active

🤧 Cold & Flu Prevention:
• Avoid close contact with sick people
• Cover mouth when coughing/sneezing
• Boost immunity with vitamin C rich foods
```

## 🎯 Response Quality Comparison

### Query: "What are dengue symptoms?"

#### BEFORE:
```
"Dengue causes fever and body pain. See a doctor if you have symptoms."
```
**Issues:**
- Too brief
- No specific symptoms
- No prevention advice
- No warning signs

#### AFTER:
```
🔍 Direct Answer:
Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection common during rainy season.

⚠️ Key Symptoms:
• High fever (104°F/40°C) lasting 2-7 days
• Severe headache
• Pain behind the eyes
• Joint and muscle pain (breakbone fever)
• Nausea and vomiting
• Skin rash (appears 2-5 days after fever)
• Mild bleeding (nose, gums)

💡 Prevention:
• Eliminate standing water (mosquito breeding sites)
• Use mosquito repellent (DEET-based)
• Wear long sleeves and pants
• Use mosquito nets while sleeping
• Keep surroundings clean and dry

⚡ Quick Actions:
• Monitor temperature every 4 hours
• Stay hydrated with ORS or coconut water
• Rest completely
• Avoid aspirin and ibuprofen (bleeding risk)

🏥 URGENT - See Doctor Immediately If:
• Severe abdominal pain
• Persistent vomiting
• Bleeding from nose or gums
• Blood in vomit or stool
• Difficulty breathing
• Extreme fatigue or restlessness
• Rapid drop in platelet count

⚕️ This is for awareness only. Dengue can be life-threatening. 
Please consult a healthcare professional immediately if you 
suspect dengue infection.
```

**Improvements:**
✅ Comprehensive symptom list
✅ Prevention strategies
✅ Warning signs clearly marked
✅ Actionable advice
✅ Urgent care indicators
✅ Medical disclaimer
